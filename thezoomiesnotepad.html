📜 The Rules of Chaos

Welcome to The Zoomies Notepad. Your goal is to work. The cat's goal is to ruin your work.

  • Do Not Stop: Stop typing, and the cat steps on your keyboard.
  • Earn Bribes: Type continuously to earn Cat Treats.
  • Spend Bribes: Feed the cat for guaranteed peace, buy permanent cosmetics, or gamble them away on mystery boxes.

🧠 Why subject yourself to this?

  • Cure Writer's Block: Panic is the ultimate muse. You can't overthink your prose when fighting for your life.
  • Practice Speed: Learn to type faster than your own anxiety.
  • Build Confidence: If you can write an essay under these hostile conditions, you can write anywhere.
